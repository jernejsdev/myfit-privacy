Privacy Policy – Setwise

We keep this policy concise and readable. If anything is unclear, contact us—we’ll help.

We never sell your data. We only use it to operate and improve Setwise.
#

1. Information We Collect

Setwise only collects the minimum data required to provide core functionality:

  • Email address (for login and account management)
  • Hashed password
  • Workout logs and history
  • Optional user feedback (including attachments)

We do not collect sensitive categories (health diagnoses, political opinions, etc.).

#

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the app experience. We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except trusted service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., hosting and authentication).

  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • Sync your workouts and preferences across devices.
  • Diagnose bugs and prevent abuse (basic logs).
  • Respond when you contact support.
#

3. Lawful Bases (GDPR/EEA)

If you are in the EU/EEA, our processing is based on:

  • Contract: to provide the Setwise service you've requested.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure the service and improve performance.
  • Consent: for optional features where required (we'll ask clearly first).
#

4. Data Security

All sensitive information (such as passwords) is securely hashed/encrypted in transit and at rest where applicable. We apply industry‑standard practices to protect your data.

#

5. Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time (see below). Backups and logs are kept for a limited time for security and recovery, after which they are purged.

#

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Receive a copy of your data (portability).
  • Withdraw consent where processing relies on consent.

To exercise any right, contact us at jernejsdev@gmail.com. EU users can lodge a complaint with the Informacijski pooblaščenec (Slovenian Information Commissioner) or your local authority.

#

7. International Transfers

If data is processed outside your country, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., contracts with standard clauses) to protect it according to applicable law.

#

8. Account & Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us:

Email: jernejsdev@gmail.com

All data will be permanently removed within 30 days of your request, subject to necessary security backups that are purged on their regular cycle.

#

9. Children’s Privacy

Setwise is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us to remove it.

#

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the app or legal requirements. We will update the “Last updated” date and, if changes are significant, provide a notice in‑app.

#

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: jernejsdev@gmail.com
